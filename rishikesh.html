Rishikesh Trip Itinerary

01

Vadodara to Rishikesh

Assemble at Vadodara station/airport in the afternoon. Board train/flight towards Haridwar/Rishikesh. Overnight journey.

Meals: Dinner

02

Arrival & Ganga Aarti

Arrive in Rishikesh. Check-in to hotel and rest. In the evening, attend the famous Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

03

Adventure & River Rafting

Enjoy thrilling white-water river rafting in the Ganga. Later visit Laxman Jhula & Ram Jhula. Evening free for cafes.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

04

Neelkanth Mahadev Temple & Ashrams

Morning visit to Neelkanth Mahadev Temple. Later, explore Parmarth Niketan Ashram and yoga/meditation sessions.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

05

Excursion to Haridwar

Day trip to Haridwar. Visit Har Ki Pauri, attend evening Aarti, and enjoy shopping at local bazaars. Return to Rishikesh.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

06

Return to Vadodara

Check out after breakfast. Depart from Rishikesh by afternoon/evening train/flight. Arrive Vadodara by night. Trip ends.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
